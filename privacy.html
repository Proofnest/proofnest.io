Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

ProofNest is an open-source Python library for verifiable AI decisions. This privacy policy explains how we handle data on proofnest.io.

TL;DR: We only collect analytics if you consent. The ProofNest library itself runs locally and doesn't send data anywhere.

What We Collect

If you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand:

  • Which pages are visited
  • How visitors found us
  • General geographic region (country level)
  • Device type and browser

We enable IP anonymization, so your full IP address is never stored.

Third-Party Services

This website uses:

  • Google Fonts - for typography (Space Grotesk, JetBrains Mono)
  • Google Analytics 4 - for anonymous usage statistics (only with your consent)

Cookies

We use localStorage to remember your cookie preference. If you accept analytics cookies, Google Analytics sets its own cookies to track visits.

You can clear these at any time in your browser settings.

The ProofNest Library

The ProofNest Python package runs entirely on your machine. It does not:

  • Send telemetry or usage data
  • Phone home to any server
  • Collect any personal information

Bitcoin anchoring (via OpenTimestamps) is optional and only happens when you explicitly call the anchoring functions.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

  • Decline analytics cookies
  • Request deletion of any data we have
  • Access information about what we've collected
