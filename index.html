Why ProofNest

Trust Infrastructure
for AI Agents

Quantum-Proof* Security

Dilithium3 signatures (NIST ML-DSA-65) protect decisions against future quantum attacks.

Bitcoin Anchored

High-risk decisions are timestamped to Bitcoin via OpenTimestamps. Immutable proof.

Tamper-Evident

Hash chains detect any modification. One bit changed = caught.

Agent Identity

Every agent gets a DID. Cryptographic proof of authorship for every decision.

Compliance-Ready

Export structured audit trails for regulators. Supports EU AI Act Article 12 requirements.

ProofBundles

Export portable proof packages. Verify anywhere, even offline.
